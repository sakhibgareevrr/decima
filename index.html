Petrophysics

Petrophysics studies physical and chemical properties of the rock and saturating fluids. Engineer calculates properties related to rock pore system, fluid distribution and flow characteristics. Here is few properties that can be evaluated: thickness, rock type, porosity, fluid type, fluid saturation, pressures, fluid phase permeability.

Reservoir

Reservoir Engineering studies and finds the most economic way of producing the reservoir during its whole life, from the exploration to abandonment phases. At any stage in reservoir development the main question is how much the reservoir can still ecomomically produce so the forecast is one of typical products.

TX Board of Professional Engineers

Under the Texas Engineering Practice Act, only duly licensed persons may legally perform, or offer to perform engineering services for the public. To be eligible for a professional engineering license, engineers must have achieved certain professional milestones. They must have earned an engineering education, performed certain levels of engineering work experience, and passed specific examinations. If not in Texas please contact your state board of professional engineers for further details.
